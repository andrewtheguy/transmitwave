ℹ️ How to use:
1. Click "Start Listening" to begin microphone capture
2. Play encoded audio from your device or another speaker near the microphone
3. The detector will show when it detects a postamble (end-of-frame)
4. Adjust the threshold to control sensitivity (lower = more sensitive)
5. View all detections in the list below
🎵 Postamble Details:
• Descending chirp from 4000Hz to 200Hz
• Duration: 250ms (4000 samples at 16kHz)
• Detection method: Cross-correlation with Pearson coefficient
• Purpose: Marks the end of the data frame
• Threshold range: 0.0 (always detect) to 1.0 (never detect)
• Recommended threshold: 0.4 for balanced detection
